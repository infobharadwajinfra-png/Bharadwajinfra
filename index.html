About Us

Established in 2023, Bharadwaj Nirman (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. is based in Rewa, delivering professional expertise in Civil Engineering, Road Construction, Electrical Installations, Building Works, and Support Services. We undertake both Government and Private contracts with an emphasis on quality, reliability and sustainable execution.

In 2024, we registered officially as Bharadwaj Nirman (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. with a dedicated team of approximately 15 employees, enabling us to participate in large-scale Government and Private tenders.
