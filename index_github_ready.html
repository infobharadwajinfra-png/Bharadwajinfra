About Us

Bharadwaj Infra & Services (BIS)

Founder & Director: Mr. Ashish Kumar Dwivedi

Bharadwaj Infra & Services (BIS), led by Mr. Ashish Kumar Dwivedi, is a professional service firm operating across Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh. We specialize in reliable, tender-based solutions for municipal bodies and private organizations, ensuring quality, safety and timely completion of projects.

Mission: To deliver dependable services with professionalism, quality, and punctuality, building long-term trust with our clients.

Values: Trust • Honesty • Professionalism • Quality
